About FocusHive

Focus Hive is an innovative web-based learning platform developed to improve focus,
motivation, and productivity for students and professionals by integrating the
Pomodoro time management technique with virtual collaboration tools. With the rise
of online education and remote work, many individuals struggle with maintaining
concentration, managing time effectively, and staying motivated in isolated study
environments. Focus Hive addresses these challenges by providing users with the
ability to create or join structured study sessions, either individually or in groups,
supported by real-time audio and video communication using WebRTC technology.
